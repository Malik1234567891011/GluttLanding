How It Works

Three steps from inspiration to dinner.

1) Import what you find

Save recipes from TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, web links, or screenshots directly into Glutt.

2) Plan with your pantry

Glutt checks what you already have, spots missing ingredients, and helps organize your week.

3) Cook with confidence

Follow guided steps, get practical AI adjustments, and finish meals you actually eat.
