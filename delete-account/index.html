Delete Account

You can request deletion of your Glutt account and associated personal data at any time.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to hi@cielpm.ai from the email linked to your Glutt account.
  2. Use the subject line: "Delete my account".
  3. Include any details that help us verify the request.

What happens next

  • We will confirm receipt of your request by email.
  • After verification, we will process account deletion within a reasonable timeframe.
  • Some limited records may be retained where legally required.

Need help?

If you cannot access your account email, contact hi@cielpm.ai and we will guide you through verification.
